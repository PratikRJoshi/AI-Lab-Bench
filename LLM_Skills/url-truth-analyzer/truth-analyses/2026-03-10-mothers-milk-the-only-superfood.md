## Notable Pregnancy Statistics Verification
- "Uterus grows from 60g to 1.1 kg" — Supported (60g non-pregnant; 1,000–1,200g at term)
- "Blood volume increases 1.5 L (~45%)" — Supported (increases from ~3.5 L to ~5–5.5 L)
- "Resting metabolic rate +60 kcal/day by week 4" — Supported (incremental throughout; total increase +300–500 kcal/day by term)
- "Uterus expands to 500× original volume" — Supported (volume increases ~500× from ~5 mL to ~5 L)
- "450 Newtons force per uterine contraction during labor" — Supported (~45 kg equivalent; documented in obstetric literature)
- "Labor burns ~597 kcal" — Supported (variable by labor length; typical active labor ~500–600 kcal)
